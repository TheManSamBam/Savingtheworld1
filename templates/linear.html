LINEAR EQUATIONS

A linear equation is an equation in which the highest power of the variable is always 1. It is also known as a one-degree equation. The standard form of a linear equation in one variable is of the form Ax + B = y. Here, x is a variable, A is a coefficient and B is constant. In the example (y = -x + 5) above -1 is the coefficient, 5 is the constant and x is the variable. In the example 2 (y = 1/2x + 2) above 1/2 is the coefficient, 2 is the constant and x is the variable.
